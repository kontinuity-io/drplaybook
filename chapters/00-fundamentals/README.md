# Chapter 00: DR Fundamentals

**Through-line:** Most DR programmes fail not because engineers are incompetent, but because they're measuring the wrong things — or measuring nothing at all. This chapter gives you the language, mental models, and baseline vocabulary that everything else in the manual builds on.

**What you build:** By the end of this chapter you have a completed RPO/RTO worksheet for your organisation's top-tier workloads, a Recovery Group mapping draft, and a tier classification matrix.

**Prerequisites:** None.
