# Chapter 01: Designing a DR Programme

**Through-line:** Knowing your RPO and RTO targets is necessary but not sufficient. This chapter teaches you to design a DR architecture that can actually deliver those targets — from topology selection through dependency mapping to policy documentation.

**What you build:** A topology decision for your environment, a dependency map for your top-tier application, and a DR policy document ready for sign-off.

**Prerequisites:** Chapter 00 complete.
