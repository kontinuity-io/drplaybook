# Chapter 02: Replication Technologies

**Through-line:** Replication is the mechanism; DR is the outcome. This chapter teaches you how each major replication technology works, what metrics to watch, and — critically — what each technology can and cannot guarantee in a failover scenario.

**What you build:** A technology assessment for each replication product in your environment, and a decision framework for choosing between them.

**Prerequisites:** Chapter 00 complete.
