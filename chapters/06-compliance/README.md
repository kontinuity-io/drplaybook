# Chapter 06: Compliance Evidence

**Through-line:** SAMA, NCA, ISO 22301, DORA, and RBI/SEBI all require DR evidence. This chapter teaches you to produce that evidence — not from documentation, but from your actual DR programme. Every lesson in this chapter produces an artifact that maps directly to a compliance control.

**What you build:** A compliance evidence pack per regulation, ready for auditor submission.

**Prerequisites:** Chapters 00–03 complete.

**Language note:** Arabic translations available for SAMA and NCA lessons (GCC-specific regulations).
